About Us

At the Islamic and Cultural Association of Bedfordview. We’re dedicated to fostering a vibrant and inclusive Muslim community where cultural understanding and mutual respect flourish. Our mission is to create a welcoming space for people from all walks of life to come together, learn, and grow through a variety of enriching activities and services. We believe in the power of community and the importance of building strong, supportive relationships. We offer educational programs, 5 daily Jamat, Madressah (Islamic schooling), and we have a ladies section for our sisters in Bedfordview, while creating a more inclusive and understanding environment. Let’s work together to foster more devoutness within our Bedfordview Ummah.
