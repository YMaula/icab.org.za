Upcoming Holidays

Lailat al Miraj:

Date: January 27, 2025

Date: 27 Rajab 1446

Commemorates the Prophet Muhammad's journey from Mecca to Jerusalem and his ascension to the heavens.

Lailat al Bara'ah:

Date: February 14, 2025

Date: 15 Shaban 1446

The Night of Forgiveness and Blessings, observed on the 15th day of the month of Sha'ban.

Ramadan:

Date: Begins March 1 (or 2), 2025

Date: 1 Ramadan 1446

The ninth and the holiest month of the Islamic calendar, a month of fasting, prayer, and reflection.

Laylat al-Qadr: March 27, 2025 -

Date: March (21st, 23rd 25th, 27th, or 29th, nights), 2025

Date: 21st, 23rd, 25th, 27th or 29th Ramadan 1446

The Night of Power, believed to be the night when the Holy Quran was first revealed to Prophet Muhammad SAW.

Eid al-Fitr:

Date: March 30 (or 31), 2025

Date: 1 Shawwal 1446

The "Festival of Breaking the Fast," marking the end of Ramadan.

Waqf al-Arafa:

Date: June 5th, 6th, and 7th, 2025

Date: 9 Dhul Hijjah 1446

The Day of Arafa, observed on the ninth day of the month of Dhu al-Hijjah during the Hajj pilgrimage.

Eid al-Adha:

Date: June 6th or 7th, 2025

Date: 10 Dhul Hijjah 1446

The "Festival of Sacrifice," commemorating the willingness of Ibrahim (AS) to sacrifice his son Ismail (AS) as an act of obedience to Allah Ta'alah.

Islamic New Year:

Date: June 26, 2025

Date: 1 Muharram 1447

The first day of the Islamic calendar, marking the beginning of a new year.
