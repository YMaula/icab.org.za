About the Project

The Bedfordview Musjid Project is set to commence shortly, Inshaallah. This marks a significant milestone for our community as we embark on the construction of a new musjid that will serve as a beacon of faith, unity, and support for all. This sacred endeavor will be funded entirely through Lillah contributions, ensuring that every bit of support goes directly into creating a space that fosters spiritual growth, community bonding, and charitable outreach. Our vision is to build not just a place of worship, but a hub for communal activities, educational programs, and social services. This project will be a testament to our collective dedication and commitment to preserving and nurturing our faith for future generations. We invite everyone to be a part of this historic journey, contributing in any way they can, whether through donations, volunteering, or spreading the word. Stay tuned for more updates on our progress and how you can get involved. Let us come together in this noble cause and build a sanctuary that stands as a symbol of our shared values and aspirations. May Allah bless this endeavor and all those who support it..
